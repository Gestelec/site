    Your Partner for a Green Future

    At Gestelec, we specialize in providing top-notch electrical services and sustainable green energy solutions. Our mission is to power a greener future through innovative and eco-friendly practices. Join us in making the world a brighter, more sustainable place.

    Comprehensive Electrical Solutions

    We specialize in power cabinets, controls, distributions, and regulations, with wiring done in our workshop. Our services include installations of interior, exterior, and public lighting to enhance lighting performance while reducing electrical consumption. We handle all types of electrical panels, whether for industry, tertiary, new constructions, residential projects, or renovations. Additionally, we offer home automation solutions for any scenario and the installation of grounding networks and protection against lightning.

    Our Partners

    At Gestelec, we collaborate with leading industry partners to deliver superior electrical solutions. Our alliances ensure access to advanced technologies and sustainable practices. Together, we tackle complex projects and drive innovation. By leveraging our partners' expertise, we enhance our service quality. These partnerships enable us to exceed client expectations consistently.
